About Us


Cakes by LIS whips up customized cakes for all types of celebration. Miss Lilian Serna, a retired Government Service Official started Cakes by LIS as a retirement hobby. She is fond of testing out new cake flavors and would let her relatives and friends be taste-testers. Eventually, her hobby became a full-blown business that serves stunning cake designs and magnificent flavors that leave smiles on the customers' faces.
