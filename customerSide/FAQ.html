Frequently Asked Questions

What is the price of a cake?
The price of custom-made wedding or other celebration cake depends on several factors, among them: the number and sizes of cake tiers, the intricacy of the cake design/decoration, and cake flavor. I am providing you with our current price list showing the indicative prices based on different cake sizes. I can give you a proper price quote once you give us your desired cake specifications and design pegs. We can also help you with the design of your cake.
How do I place a cake order?
Please fill up the Order/Contract form provided to you. After we have finalized the details, and signed the Order/Contract Form, we will book your order as soon as you have paid the required 5% nonrefundable deposit which forms part of the cake price.
How do I make my payment? Do you require a deposit?
As indicated in the Order/Contract Form which we will execute, we will book your order upon your payment of a 5% nonrefundible deposit. The balance should be paid no later than 3 days before cake pick-up (for single-tier cakes); and at least 1-week before pick-up/delivery of multilayered cakes. The 5% deposit assures you of a slot in our calendar of orders. Payments can be made to our specified GCash or Bank Accounts.
How many days advance notice do you require for cake orders.
We can take only a limited number of orders a week, so we advice you to place your order soon after you have set the date for your special occasion. Provided your desired date is still open (in our cake order calendar), we require at least a week's notice (confirmed order) for single-layer cake and a month for multi-tier cakes.
Do you deliver to areas outside of Metro Manila? How much is the delivery fee?
Yes, we can deliver to nearby cities outside MM, but only for multilayer cakes. We charge a minimal delivery fee depending on the distance of the celebration venue from our place in Manila. Single-tier cakes, however, are only for pick-up.
What cake flavors do you offer?
Some of the favorite flavors ordered from us are Carrot-Walnut, Citrus Poundcake (w/ glazed fruits & walnuts), Chocofudge-Caramel, Banana-Cashew and Coffee-Caramel Poundcake but we also make other flavors preferred by clients. Each cake tier can be a different flavor.
What is the process of ordering and payment?
  1. Fill up the Personal Information and Cake Customization Form.
  2. After submitting the order, wait for one of our staff to contact you through the contact details you've provided. If none, you can contact them through the details provided on the footer.
  3. During the conversation, finalize the cake customization and final price. Add other cake features that were not provided by the form.
  4. Pay the down payment either through Gcash or Bank Transfer. It will also indicate that you've agreed to the final price of the cake.
  5. Wait for the company to contact you again for the full payment.
  6. After full payment, wait for the delivery, depending on the method you've chosen, then notify the company that you've already received the cake. If by pick-up, it is not needed.
